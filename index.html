Structure:

1. Listening and collecting information

Negotiation starts before you talk about price. This section goes over how to go about knowing what your customers want, what to ask them about, and how to distill that information in an easy to digest format.

2. Value creation

Many people talk about value creation like it is some checkmark on a list where you "synergize your paradigms". In this module you will learn that value creation is using what you have learned about your customer to then create stellar offers full of perceived value. To overflow your customers value bucket.

3. Tactics

This section is last for a reason. Tactics are only useful with a foundation in value and listening. Here you will learn how to negotiate on price. Also you will learn about how to be your customers partner not employee, and why you should never lower your price for the same value.

After you complete this course you will never:

  1. Lower your price without lowering your value
  2. Be treated as an employee
  3. End up negotiating with the HR department on a new contract
  4. Negotiate on price without researching their issues first

  • Ways to communicate the value of the product or service you provide--when ROI cannot be calculated
  • How to separate non-buyers with the buyers
